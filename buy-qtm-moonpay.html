Flow overview

From card to QTM in two clear stages

The process is deliberately simple: first you use MoonPay to acquire MATIC directly on the Polygon network using card or local methods; then you use a decentralised exchange to swap that MATIC into QTM.

  1. Click “Start MoonPay purchase” above, choose your fiat currency, and complete the MoonPay KYC and payment steps.
  2. Ensure the destination wallet you provide inside MoonPay is a Polygon‑enabled address that you personally control (for example, a MetaMask wallet with the Polygon network configured).
  3. Once MATIC arrives, return to this page and select your preferred DEX below (Uniswap, ApeSwap, Sushi) with the QTM pair pre‑configured.
  4. Execute a swap from MATIC into QTM directly from your self‑custodied wallet, keeping full control of your keys and assets at every step.
